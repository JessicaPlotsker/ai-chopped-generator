Here's a creative recipe that combines ramen, corn, apples, and green beans into a delicious and unique dish: **Sweet and Savory Ramen Stir-Fry**.

### Ingredients:
- 2 packs of instant ramen (discard seasoning packets)
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned and drained)
- 1 medium apple, cored and sliced thinly (choose a firm variety like Fuji or Granny Smith)
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder (or fresh grated ginger)
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (or fresh minced garlic)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

### Instructions:

1. **Cook the Ramen:**
   - Boil the ramen noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.

2. **Prepare the Stir-Fry Sauce:**
   - In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, hoisin sauce, ginger, and garlic powder.

3. **Stir-Fry the Vegetables:**
   - Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
   - Add the green beans and corn kernels to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the green beans are tender-crisp.
   - Add the apple slices to the skillet and stir-fry for another 2 minutes until the apples are slightly softened.

4. **Combine Ingredients:**
   - Add the cooked ramen noodles to the skillet with the vegetables and apple.
   - Pour the stir-fry sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss everything together to coat evenly. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the noodles are heated through and all ingredients are well combined.

5. **Season and Serve:**
   - Taste and season with salt and pepper, if needed.
   - Serve the stir-fry hot, garnished with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

This Sweet and Savory Ramen Stir-Fry makes for a delightful fusion dish with a balance of textures and flavors from the crunchy green beans, sweet corn and apples, and hearty ramen noodles. Enjoy!
