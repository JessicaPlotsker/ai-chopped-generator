Creating a dish with oats, olives, cheese, and sweet potatoes might seem unusual at first, but these ingredients can come together in a savory and hearty baked casserole. Here's a recipe you can try:

### Savory Oat and Sweet Potato Casserole

#### Ingredients:
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
- 1/2 cup pitted olives, sliced (green or black, based on preference)
- 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or feta work well)
- 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh herbs for garnish (optional, such as parsley or basil)

#### Instructions:

1. **Preheat Oven:**
   - Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Prepare Sweet Potatoes:**
   - In a pot, bring water to a boil and add the diced sweet potatoes. Cook for about 10 minutes until they are just tender, then drain and set aside.

3. **Cook Aromatics:**
   - In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
   - Stir in the garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

4. **Mix Ingredients:**
   - In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked onions and garlic, sweet potatoes, oats, sliced olives, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
   - Add the broth to the mixture and stir well to combine all the ingredients.

5. **Assemble Casserole:**
   - Transfer the mixture into a greased baking dish.
   - Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top.

6. **Bake:**
   - Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
   - Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly with a slight golden color.

7. **Serve:**
   - Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
   - Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

This casserole is perfect as a side dish or a light main course. The oats provide a nutty texture, the sweet potatoes add sweetness, olives offer a briny contrast, and cheese binds everything together with its creamy, rich flavor. Enjoy!
