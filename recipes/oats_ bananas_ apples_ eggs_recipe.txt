You can create a delicious and healthy Baked Oatmeal Casserole using oats, bananas, apples, and eggs. Here's a simple recipe:

### Baked Oatmeal Casserole

#### Ingredients:
- 2 cups rolled oats
- 2 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1 apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk (or any non-dairy milk)
- 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- Optional: 1/4 cup chopped nuts (like walnuts or pecans)

#### Instructions:
1. **Preheat Oven:** Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease an 8x8 inch baking dish or a similar-sized casserole dish.

2. **Mix Wet Ingredients:** In a large bowl, whisk together the mashed bananas, eggs, milk, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract until well combined.

3. **Add Dry Ingredients:** Stir in the oats, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt until everything is mixed. Fold in the chopped apples.

4. **Prepare to Bake:** Pour the mixture into your prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly. If you're using nuts, sprinkle them over the top.

5. **Bake:** Place the dish in the oven and bake for about 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the oatmeal is set.

6. **Cool and Serve:** Allow the casserole to cool slightly before serving. You can enjoy it warm or at room temperature.

#### Serving Suggestions:
- Serve with a dollop of yogurt or a drizzle of additional honey or maple syrup.
- Add a sprinkle of cinnamon or a handful of fresh fruit on top for extra flavor.

This dish is perfect for breakfast or a healthy snack and is easy to prepare in advance to enjoy throughout the week!
