### Apple-Spinach Ramen Stir-Fry

#### Ingredients:
- 1 package of ramen noodles
- 1 cup fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 apple, thinly sliced (preferably a sweeter variety like Fuji or Gala)
- 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

#### Instructions:

1. **Cook the Ramen:**
   - In a pot of boiling water, cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. **Prepare the Green Beans:**
   - In a large pan or wok, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the green beans and sauté for about 4-5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

3. **Add Garlic and Ginger:**
   - Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan. Stir for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

4. **Add Apple Slices:**
   - Add the apple slices to the pan and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.

5. **Stir in Spinach:**
   - Add the spinach leaves to the pan and stir until they are wilted.

6. **Combine Noodles and Sauce:**
   - Add the cooked ramen noodles to the pan. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil, tossing everything together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

7. **Serve:**
   - Transfer the stir-fry to serving plates. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired.

This dish is a great blend of flavors and textures, combining the earthiness of the spinach and green beans with the sweet crispness of the apple, all tied together by the umami of the soy sauce and sesame oil. Enjoy your meal!
