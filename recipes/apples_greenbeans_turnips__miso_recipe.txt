### Miso-Glazed Turnips and Green Beans with Apple Salad

#### Ingredients:
- **For the Glazed Turnips and Green Beans:**
  - 2 medium turnips, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  - 200g fresh green beans, trimmed
  - 2 tablespoons white miso paste
  - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  - 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  - 2 tablespoons water
  - 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  - 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

- **For the Apple Salad:**
  - 1 apple, thinly sliced
  - 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - A handful of fresh arugula or spinach

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Miso Glaze:**
   - In a small bowl, mix together the miso paste, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey (or maple syrup), sesame oil, and water until smooth. Set aside.

2. **Cook the Turnips:**
   - Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
   - Add the turnip pieces and cook until they begin to soften and turn golden brown, about 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. **Add the Green Beans:**
   - Add the green beans to the skillet with the turnips and stir to combine.
   - Pour the miso glaze over the vegetables and toss to coat them evenly.
   - Cover the skillet, reduce heat to low, and let it cook for another 5–7 minutes, or until the green beans are tender but still crisp and the turnips are fully cooked through.

4. **Prepare the Apple Salad:**
   - In a large bowl, toss the apple slices with lemon juice to prevent browning.
   - Add the olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
   - Add the arugula or spinach and toss gently to combine.

5. **Serve:**
   - Plate the miso-glazed turnips and green beans, and garnish with sesame seeds if using.
   - Serve with a side of the fresh apple salad.

This dish offers a balance of sweet, savory, and umami flavors from the miso glaze paired with the freshness and tartness of the apple salad. Enjoy your culinary creation!
