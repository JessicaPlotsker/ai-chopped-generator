### Green Bean, Corn, and Citrus Salad

#### Ingredients:
- 1 cup fresh green beans, trimmed and halved
- 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
- 4 cups lettuce, chopped (Romaine or your preferred variety)
- 2 large oranges, peeled and segmented
- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced (optional)
- 1/4 cup almonds or walnuts, toasted (optional)

#### Dressing:
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed orange juice
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
- Salt and pepper to taste

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Green Beans:** 
   - Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and the green beans. Blanch for about 3-4 minutes until they are tender yet crisp.
   - Drain and immediately transfer the green beans to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain again once cooled.

2. **Prepare the Corn:**
   - If using fresh corn, boil it for about 3-5 minutes, then cut the kernels off the cob. If using frozen corn, thaw it; and if using canned corn, drain it well.

3. **Make the Dressing:** 
   - In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, orange juice, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined.

4. **Assemble the Salad:**
   - In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped lettuce, green beans, corn, orange segments, and red onion if using.
   - Toss the salad with the prepared dressing until everything is well coated.

5. **Serve:**
   - Sprinkle the toasted almonds or walnuts over the salad for added crunch and flavor, if desired. 
   - Serve immediately as a refreshing side or a light main course.

This salad offers a wonderful balance of flavors and textures, with the citrusy notes of the oranges complementing the freshness of the green beans, corn, and lettuce.
