### Mixed Fruit Salad

#### Ingredients:
- 1 apple, cored and diced
- 1 pear, cored and diced
- 1 cup of grapes, halved
- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (to prevent browning and add a touch of tang)
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
- A handful of fresh mint leaves, chopped (optional, for garnish)

#### Instructions:
1. **Prepare the Fruits:**
   - In a large bowl, combine the diced apple, diced pear, halved grapes, and sliced banana.

2. **Prevent Browning:**
   - Drizzle the lemon juice over the mixed fruits and gently toss to coat. This will prevent the fruits, especially the apple and banana, from browning.

3. **Add Sweetness (Optional):**
   - If you prefer a sweeter fruit salad, drizzle the honey or maple syrup over the fruits and toss again to distribute evenly.

4. **Garnish:**
   - Sprinkle the chopped mint leaves over the salad for an extra burst of freshness and a pop of color.

5. **Chill and Serve:**
   - You can serve the salad immediately or chill it in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to let the flavors meld together. Either way, it will be refreshing and delicious!

This fruit salad is perfect as a light snack, a side dish, or even a healthy dessert. Enjoy!
