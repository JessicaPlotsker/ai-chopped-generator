### Salmon with Mango-Mushroom Brown Rice

#### Ingredients:
- 2 salmon fillets
- 1 cup brown rice
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms (such as cremini or button)
- 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish
- Lime wedges for serving

#### Instructions:

1. **Cook the Brown Rice:**
   - Rinse the brown rice under cold water.
   - In a medium saucepan, combine the rice with 2 cups of water and a pinch of salt.
   - Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Cook for about 45 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.
   - Fluff with a fork and set aside.

2. **Prepare the Marinade for Salmon:**
   - In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, and ginger.
   - Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them.
   - Allow to marinate for at least 15 minutes.

3. **Cook the Mushrooms:**
   - In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
   - Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent.
   - Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are browned and tender, about 5-7 minutes.
   - Season with salt and pepper.

4. **Combine Brown Rice and Mushrooms:**
   - Add the cooked brown rice to the skillet with the mushrooms.
   - Stir in the diced mango and mix everything well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.

5. **Cook the Salmon:**
   - In another pan or grill, cook the salmon fillets over medium-high heat for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your liking.

6. **Assemble the Dish:**
   - Divide the mango-mushroom rice mixture onto plates.
   - Top each serving with a salmon fillet.
   - Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley and serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.

Enjoy your flavorful and healthy meal!
