Creating a dish that combines ramen, apples, oranges, and asparagus might seem unusual, but it can result in a delightful and innovative fusion meal. Here's a recipe for a Ramen Salad with Citrus Dressing:

### Ramen Salad with Citrus Dressing

#### Ingredients:

- **For the Salad:**
  - 1 pack of ramen noodles (discard the seasoning packet)
  - 1 cup asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
  - 1 apple, thinly sliced (choose a crisp variety like Fuji or Granny Smith)
  - Segments from 1 orange (or use canned mandarin oranges for convenience)
  - 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  - A handful of fresh mint or cilantro, chopped (optional)

- **For the Citrus Dressing:**
  - Juice of 1 orange
  - Zest of 1 orange
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  - 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - Salt and pepper to taste

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Noodles:**
   - Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions, omitting the seasoning packet. Drain and set aside to cool.

2. **Blanch the Asparagus:**
   - Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Add the asparagus pieces and blanch for about 2 minutes, until they're bright green and tender-crisp.
   - Immediately transfer the asparagus to an ice bath or run under cold water to stop the cooking process. Drain and set aside.

3. **Make the Dressing:**
   - In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, orange zest, olive oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

4. **Assemble the Salad:**
   - In a large bowl, combine the cooked and cooled ramen noodles, asparagus, apple slices, orange segments, and green onions.
   - Pour the citrus dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine, ensuring everything is coated with the dressing.

5. **Garnish and Serve:**
   - Garnish with chopped mint or cilantro if desired.
   - Serve immediately as a refreshing, light meal or side dish. Enjoy!

This salad blends the chewiness of ramen noodles with the crispness of apples and asparagus, complemented by the sweet and tangy citrus dressing. It's a vibrant and refreshing dish, perfect for a unique culinary adventure!
