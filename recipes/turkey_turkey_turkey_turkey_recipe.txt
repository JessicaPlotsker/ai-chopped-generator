### Turkey Extravaganza

#### Ingredients:
- **Turkey Breast**: 1 large piece
- **Ground Turkey**: 1 pound
- **Turkey Sausage**: 4 links
- **Smoked Turkey**: 1 cup, diced

#### For the Marinade (for Turkey Breast):
- Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
- Lemon juice: 2 tablespoons
- Garlic powder: 1 teaspoon
- Paprika: 1 teaspoon
- Salt and pepper to taste

#### Additional Ingredients:
- Onion: 1, chopped
- Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
- Bell peppers: 2, assorted colors, sliced
- Fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary, or parsley): to garnish
- Optional: Gravy or cranberry sauce for serving

### Instructions:

#### 1. Marinate and Roast the Turkey Breast:
1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
2. Coat the turkey breast in the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
4. Place the marinated turkey breast on a baking sheet and roast for about 45 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
5. Let it rest before slicing.

#### 2. Prepare the Ground Turkey Meatballs:
1. In a bowl, combine ground turkey with salt, pepper, and half of the minced garlic. You can add breadcrumbs and a beaten egg for binding.
2. Form the mixture into small meatballs.
3. Pan-fry or bake the meatballs until they are fully cooked and browned on all sides.

#### 3. Cook the Turkey Sausage:
1. In a skillet, cook the turkey sausages over medium heat until they are browned and cooked through, about 10 minutes.
2. Slice them into bite-sized pieces.

#### 4. Sauté the Smoked Turkey with Vegetables:
1. In the same skillet, add a little olive oil and sauté onions and the remaining garlic until translucent.
2. Add sliced bell peppers and smoked turkey, cooking until the peppers soften.

### Assembly:
1. Arrange sliced roasted turkey, turkey meatballs, turkey sausages, and the smoked turkey with peppers on a large serving platter.
2. Garnish with fresh herbs and optionally serve with gravy or cranberry sauce on the side.

Enjoy your Turkey Extravaganza that's sure to please any turkey lover!
